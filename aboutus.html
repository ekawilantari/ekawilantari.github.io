Since 1962

Rooftop restaurant & bar at Banyan Tree Bangkok is located on the 61st floor with an outstanding 360-degree view of the Bangkok skyline and its surroundings. It is a double setting, where the restaurant part is called and the bar is called Moon Bar.

Rooftop restaurant & bar is a perfect place for a luxurious and romantic dinner. The views, service and food will get you in a superb mode directly. The seafood to premium steaks are also top notch up here. And the price is accordingly. Still, many consider this as the best among rooftop restaurants in Bangkok. Book your table in advance and you’ll be in for a treat. This will impress any date in the world.

About Our Menu

There’s nothing pretentious about our menu, but we do play with our food. Sure, you’ll find familiar ingredients, but they might be prepared in an unexpected way. If you’re vegan or vegetarian, you’ll be happy to know that we love plants almost as much as we love meat. No boring, overcooked veggies on our menu! Meat eaters and plant lovers can all live in peaceful, delicious harmony at Bar Mason.
